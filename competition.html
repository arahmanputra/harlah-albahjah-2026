Competition Categories

Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026 presents a series of competitions designed to encourage creativity, knowledge, and healthy competition among students from various educational levels.

Elementary School (SD)

Fun and educational competitions designed to build confidence, creativity, and teamwork for elementary students.

Junior High School (SMP)

Engaging challenges that promote critical thinking, knowledge, and skill development for junior high participants.

Senior High School (SMA)

Competitive and inspiring events aimed at showcasing talent, leadership, and academic excellence among senior high students.
