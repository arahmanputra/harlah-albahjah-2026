# Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026

**Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026** is the official celebration of the anniversary of Al-Bahjah.

This event is held as a moment of gratitude, unity, and togetherness. It features various activities such as competitions, religious lectures, art performances, and community programs involving students, alumni, and the wider community.

This website serves as the official information platform, providing:
- Event schedules and details  
- Competition information  
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
- Contact information  
- Latest announcements  

May Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026 strengthen unity and inspire positive contributions for everyone involved.
