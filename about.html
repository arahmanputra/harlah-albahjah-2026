About Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026

Celebrating unity, gratitude, and the spirit of togetherness.

The Meaning of Harlah

Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026 marks the anniversary of Al-Bahjah, commemorating its journey, growth, and contributions to the community. This celebration is a reflection of gratitude and collective progress.

What to Expect

The event features a series of competitions, religious lectures, artistic performances, and community activities involving students, alumni, and the wider public.

Our Vision

Harlah Al-Bahjah 2026 aims to strengthen unity, inspire positive contributions, and foster a spirit of collaboration for a brighter future.
